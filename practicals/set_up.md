## Free options to continue your learning

You can continue learning about visualizations, statistics, and reproducibility in your own laptop. These are the steps that you need to follow

1. Download all of the files from Posit.cloud in a folder locally
[How to download](downloads/downloading.png)
2. Install in your computer R and Rstudio following the instructions [here](https://rstudio-education.github.io/hopr/starting.html)
3. Open your file project.Rproj in Rstudio and voilá you can reproduce exactly the same we did in posit.cloud
